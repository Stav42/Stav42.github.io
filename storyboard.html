Log 1: A brief introduction | 11 Oct 2024

The StoryBoard is an application that is borne out of my need for effective study. I haven't yet encountered any platform where one might interact with multiple data sources simultaneously to create a meaningful synthesis out of all those, along with references. It's essentially a research tool. I wanted to be able to have multiple sources open simultaneously, pick some stuff from some source, another stuff from another source and put them together to create a story that serves my purpose. The USP of the app is going to have two components:

  • User Interface: I have an idea for the interface and it is going to be neat. The interface is extremely important and needs to be super intuitive for this application to stick
  • Effective Search: A research tool must have strong search. It's quite obvious. While the above is fairly straightforward to implement, this component is slightly more, shall I say, prone to real world complexities. Copyrights, rate limits, and quality search.
