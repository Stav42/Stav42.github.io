about me
Hi. I am Aditya Vats. I am right now working on robotics at Penn in the US. I graduated from IIT Kanpur with a B.Tech-M.Tech degree in Mechanical Engineering. I really really hope I won't need to write this after some days. I hope to create something amazing, rather than just consuming stuff. I might document some of my projects here but I will mostly be writing about things I am trying to understand. My work has primarily been in controls for robots, though I am working on other things as well. I've worked on quadruped robots, and manipulators. I have fabricated steel structures in tonnes and erected them on airport project sites. I've delved in embedded system, made robots walk through RL, made things in grams and in metric tonnes. In the future, I hope to create wonderful products that create a lot of value for society. This is my personal space, and I will be writing about things that I find interesting. I do not promise not to use LLMs, but I will mostly keep it manually written. If it is to be read by humans, then I suppose it should be written by a human :)

On a personal note, I am trying to get fitter, and have taken up my electric guitar again (after a hiatus of 3 years). Fingers crossed on consistency, hopefully I will be successful on both fronts this time around.
